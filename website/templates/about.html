About Campsite Availability Search

Welcome to Campsite Availability Search! This tool helps you find available campsites at your favorite Recreation.gov locations, making it easier to plan your outdoor adventures.

Key Features:

  • Search for campgrounds near any city in the US
  • Find available dates for specific campgrounds
  • Filter for weekend-only availability
  • View campsite locations on an interactive map
  • Quick access to Recreation.gov booking

This project is currently in development. More features and improvements are coming soon!

How to Use:

  1. Enter a city to find nearby campgrounds
  2. Click on a campground to get its Park ID
  3. Set your desired date range and preferences
  4. Click "Search Availability" to see available dates
  5. Click on any available date to book through Recreation.gov

Note: This is an independent tool and is not affiliated with Recreation.gov. All bookings are made through the official Recreation.gov website.
